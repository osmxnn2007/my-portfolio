01 About

I'm an electronics engineering student in my second year at GHRCE, spending lecture-hall hours on circuits and signals, and free hours pushing into two side disciplines: C++ programming and video editing.

Electronics gave me a way of thinking about systems — inputs, logic, outputs. C++ is the same instinct applied to software: build the circuit, then let it execute. Video editing in DaVinci Resolve and Adobe Premiere Pro is where that same structured thinking meets storytelling — timing, pacing, and signal-to-noise, just in a different medium.
